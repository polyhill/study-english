join in (doing) sth [dʒɔɪn ɪn]
参加，加入：She joined in the conversation. 她加入了谈话。
Team members joined in the brainstorming session, their creativity sparking innovative solutions.
团队成员们加入了头脑风暴会议，他们的创造力激发了创新的解决方案。
Children happily joined in the game, their laughter filling the playground.
孩子们兴高采烈地加入了游戏，他们的笑声充满了操场。
The community joined in the celebration, their spirits soaring as they shared in the festivities.
社区居民们加入了庆祝活动，他们分享着节日的气氛，精神振奋。

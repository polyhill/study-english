determine [dɪ'tɜːmɪn]
v. 决定，影响：What you eat partly determines your health. 你吃的东西某种程度上决定了你的健康。
We need to determine the answer to this math problem.
我们需要确定这个数学问题的答案。
The teacher will determine the teams for the soccer game.
老师将决定足球比赛的队伍。
It is important to determine the best way to study for the test.
确定最好的考试复习方法很重要。

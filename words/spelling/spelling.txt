spelling ['spelɪŋ]
n. 拼读；拼写：There are some spelling mistakes in his composition. 他的作文里有一些拼写错误。
Correct spelling is important for writing clear and accurate sentences.
正确的拼写对于写出清晰准确的句子很重要。
We had a fun spelling game in class where we had to spell words aloud.
我们在课堂上玩了一个有趣的拼写游戏，需要大声拼写单词。
The teacher gave us a list of words to study for the spelling test.
老师给了我们一份单词表来准备拼写测试。

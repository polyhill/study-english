transport ['trænspɔːt] 
n. 交通运输，运输：the transport of dangerous chemicals 危险化学品的运输。<br>v. 运输，运送：The oil is transported by sea to other countries. 石油是通过海运运输到其他国家的。
Transport is important for getting people and goods from one place to another.
交通对于将人和货物从一个地方运送到另一个地方非常重要。
Public transport, like buses and trains, helps reduce traffic congestion.
公共交通工具，如公交车和火车，有助于减少交通拥堵。
Improving transport infrastructure can boost the economy.
改善交通基础设施可以促进经济发展。

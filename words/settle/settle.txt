settle ['setl]
v. 解决；安家，定居：They came to France from America and finally settled in Paris. 他们从美国来到法国，最后在巴黎安居。
The traveler settled into the cozy hotel room, feeling relieved after a long journey.
旅客安顿在舒适的酒店房间里，经过长途旅行后感到放松。
The birds settled on the branches of the tree, their chirping filling the air.
鸟儿落在树枝上，它们的鸣叫声充满了空气。
The dispute was finally settled after hours of negotiation.
经过数小时的谈判，争端终于解决了。

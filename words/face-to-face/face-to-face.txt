face to face ['feɪs tə 'feɪs]
面对面：We were sitting face to face. 我们面对面坐。
We had a face to face interview with the famous author.
我们与那位著名作家进行了面对面的采访。
During the meeting, everyone shared their ideas face to face.
在会议期间，每个人都面对面地分享了他们的想法。
We talked face to face about our plans for the weekend.
我们面对面地讨论了周末的计划。

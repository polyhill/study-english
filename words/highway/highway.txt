highway ['haɪweɪ]
n. 公路；交通要道：There was a car accident on the highway. 高速公路上发生了一起车祸。
The highway was smooth and straight, making our journey fast and easy.
高速公路平坦笔直，使我们的旅途又快又轻松。
Cars zoomed past us on the busy highway, their lights flickering in the evening.
汽车在繁忙的高速公路上飞驰而过，它们的灯光在傍晚时闪烁。
At night, the highway was lit by rows of streetlights, guiding the way.
夜晚，高速公路被一排排路灯照亮，指引着方向。

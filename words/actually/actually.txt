actually ['æktʃuəli]
adv. 真实地，事实上：We thought he was lazy, but actually he was working hard. 我们以为他很懒，但事实上他很用功。
The movie was actually better than I expected.
这部电影实际上比我预期的要好。
I actually enjoy reading books more than watching TV.
实际上，我更喜欢读书而不是看电视。
Actually, I finished my homework early today.
事实上，我今天早早就完成了作业。

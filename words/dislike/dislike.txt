dislike [dɪs'laɪk]
v. 不喜欢；厌恶：He dislikes her very much. 他很不喜欢她。
I dislike getting up early, but I have to do it for school.
我不喜欢早起，但为了上学我必须这么做。
The teacher dislikes students who don't pay attention in class.
老师不喜欢上课不注意听讲的学生。
We dislike crowded places because it's hard to move around.
我们不喜欢拥挤的地方因为很难四处走动。

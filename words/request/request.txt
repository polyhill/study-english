request [rɪ'kwest]
n. 请求；要求：We've made a request for help. 我们已经请求帮助。
The librarian asked us to keep our voices down as a polite request.
图书管理员礼貌地要求我们小声一点。
My friend sent me a request to join her at the park this afternoon.
我的朋友邀请我今天下午去公园。
The teacher's request was that everyone finish their projects by Friday.
老师的要求是每个人在星期五之前完成他们的项目。

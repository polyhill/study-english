official [ə'fɪʃl]

The official school hours are from 8 AM to 3 PM.
学校的正式上课时间是从早上8点到下午3点。
The event will start after the official opening ceremony.
活动将在正式开幕仪式之后开始。
The official rules must be followed during the competition.
在比赛中必须遵守正式规则。

at present [æt 'prezənt] 
adv. 现在；目前：At present the economy is in a period of recovery. 目前经济正处在恢复期。
At present, the school is organizing a sports event.
目前，学校正在组织一场体育活动。
At present, my best friend is traveling abroad.
目前，我最好的朋友正在国外旅行。
At present, online learning has become very popular.
目前，线上学习变得非常流行。

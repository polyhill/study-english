Malaysia [mə'leɪʒə] 
n. 马来西亚
The capital city of Malaysia is Kuala Lumpur, famous for the Petronas Twin Towers.
马来西亚的首都是吉隆坡，以双子塔著称。
Malaysia is a multicultural country with diverse ethnic groups.
马来西亚是一个多元文化的国家，有各种不同的民族。
Malaysia is famous for its vibrant festivals, such as Hari Raya and Chinese New Year.
马来西亚以其丰富多彩的节日而闻名，例如开斋节和春节。

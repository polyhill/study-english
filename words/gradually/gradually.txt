gradually ['ɡrædʒuəli] 
adv. 逐渐地：His maths gradually improved. 他的数学渐渐有了提高。
As we climbed the mountain, the temperature gradually became cooler.
随着我们爬上山，气温逐渐变凉。
The snow gradually melted as the sun came out.
随着太阳出来，雪逐渐融化了。
The noise in the classroom gradually quieted down as the teacher entered.
随着老师进来，教室里的噪音逐渐安静下来。

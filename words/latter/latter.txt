latter ['lætə(r)]
n. （两者之中的）后者：He has a car and two bikes and the latter are used more. 他有一辆汽车和两辆自行车，后者更多。
Between reading and playing video games, I prefer the latter.
在读书和玩电子游戏之间，我更喜欢后者。
The first half of the movie was slow, but the latter half was very exciting.
电影的前半部分很慢，但后半部分非常精彩。
The book had many chapters, but I found the latter ones more interesting.
这本书有很多章节，但我发现后面的章节更有趣。

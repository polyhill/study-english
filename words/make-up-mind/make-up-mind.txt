make up mind 
下决心
She couldn't make up her mind whether to stay home or go out.
她拿不定主意是待在家里还是出去。
After thinking for a long time, he finally made up his mind to join the soccer team.
思考了很久之后，他终于决定加入足球队。
It took him a while to make up his mind, but he eventually chose the red bike.
他花了一段时间才下定决心，但最终选择了红色的自行车。

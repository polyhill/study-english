accent ['æksent]
n. 口音，音调：a British accent 英音<br>n. 重音：In the word 'before', the accent is on the second. 单词before的重音在第二音节。
She spoke with a charming British accent that made everyone listen carefully.
她带着迷人的英国口音说话，让大家都仔细听。
The movie had actors with various accents from around the world.
电影中的演员有来自世界各地的不同口音。
You can often tell where someone is from by their accent.
你通常可以通过一个人的口音来判断他们来自哪里。

upset [ʌp'set]
a. 难过的；沮丧的：He was very upset about his grandmother's death. 他奶奶去世了，他感到很难过。
The rainy weather made everyone feel a bit upset.
下雨的天气让每个人都感到有点沮丧。
He gets upset when his favorite team loses a game.
当他最喜欢的队伍输了比赛时，他会感到很失望。
She felt upset when she didn't get the grade she wanted on her test.
她在考试中没有得到她想要的成绩时感到很沮丧。

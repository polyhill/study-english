recognize ['rekəɡnaɪz]
vt. 认出；认识：He's changed a lot, so I hardly recognized him. 他变了好多，我几乎认不出来了。
It is important to recognize the value of teamwork in completing a project.
在完成一个项目时，认识到团队合作的重要性是很关键的。
She quickly recognized the famous actor walking down the street.
她很快认出了在街上行走的那位著名演员。
Recognizing different cultures helps us understand and respect each other better.
了解不同的文化有助于我们更好地理解和尊重彼此。

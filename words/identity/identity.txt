identity [aɪ'dentəti]
n. 身份：an ID card 身份证
Everyone's identity is different, and that's what makes the world so diverse and interesting.
每个人的身份都是不同的，这使得世界变得如此多样和有趣。
Sometimes, finding our true identity can take time and self-reflection.
有时候，找到我们真正的身份需要时间和自我反思。
Digital identity refers to the representation of a person online.
数字身份是指一个人在网上的代表。

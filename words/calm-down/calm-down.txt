calm down
（使）平静；（使）安静：She closed her eyes to calm herself down. 她闭目让自己平静下来。
The gentle breeze helped to calm down the stormy sea.
微风帮助平静了暴风雨的海。
The doctor advised the patient to calm down and avoid stress.
医生建议病人平静下来，避免压力。
After the argument, they both took some time to calm down before talking again.
争吵过后，他们都花了一些时间冷静下来才再次交谈。

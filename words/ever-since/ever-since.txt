ever since ['evə sɪns]
自从...，从...起；自从那时以后：He wanted to be a singer ever since he was a boy. 他从儿时开始就想做一名歌手。
Ever since I started reading more books, my grades have improved.
自从我开始多读书后，我的成绩就提高了。
Ever since she joined the soccer team, she has been very happy.
自从她加入足球队后，她一直非常开心。
Ever since we went to the beach last summer, I can't wait to go again.
自从我们去年夏天去了海滩后，我就迫不及待想再去一次。

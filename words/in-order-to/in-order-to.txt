in order to [ɪn 'ɔːdə tu]
prep. 为了，以便：He set out early in order to arrive on time. 他早早出发，以便按时到达。
In order to succeed in the exam, she studied diligently every night.
为了在考试中取得好成绩，她每晚都勤奋学习。
In order to maintain good health, we should exercise regularly and eat a balanced diet.
为了保持健康，我们应该经常锻炼并保持均衡饮食。
In order to solve the problem, we should analyze it carefully and break it down into smaller steps.
为了解决问题，我们应该仔细分析它并将其分解为更小的步骤。

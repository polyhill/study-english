shortcoming ['ʃɔːtkʌmɪŋ] 
n. 缺点，短处：He acknowledged his own shortcomings.
Everyone has a shortcoming, but it doesn't define who we are.
每个人都有缺点，但这并不能定义我们是谁。
Recognizing your shortcoming is the first step to improving yourself.
认识到自己的缺点是自我提升的第一步。
By working on our shortcomings, we can achieve personal growth.
通过改进我们的缺点，我们可以实现个人成长。

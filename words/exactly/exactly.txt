exactly [ɪɡ'zæktli]
adv. 精确的；确切的：I don't know exactly where he is. 我不完全清楚他在哪儿。<br> adv. 一点不错，正是如此："Your think we should accept his invitation?" "Exactly". “你认为我们应该接受他们的邀请吗？”“一点不错”
The student memorized the speech exactly, delivering it with confidence and precision.
学生完全背诵了演讲，自信而精确地发表了演讲。
The clock struck twelve exactly, signaling the start of the new year.
钟声正好敲响十二下，标志着新的一年的开始。
The chef followed the recipe exactly, ensuring that the dish turned out perfectly.
厨师严格按照食谱烹饪，确保菜肴完美呈现。

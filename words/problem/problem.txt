problem ['prɒbləm]
n. 问题，难题：solve a big problem. 解决一个大问题。<br>n. 习题，问题：no problem. 没问题。
Solving a problem requires patience and careful thinking.
解决问题需要耐心和仔细思考。
Don't give up if the first solution doesn't work; keep trying different ways to solve the problem.
如果第一个解决方案不起作用，不要放弃；继续尝试不同的方法来解决问题。
Every problem has a solution, even if it takes time to find it.
每个问题都有解决办法，只是找到它需要时间。

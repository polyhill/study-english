suggest [sə'dʒest] 
vt. 建议，提议：He suggested a walk in the park. 他建议到公园散步。<br>vt. 表明，说明：Mary's pale face suggested that she was ill. 玛丽苍白的脸表明病了。
The teacher suggested reading a book every week to improve our vocabulary.
老师建议每周读一本书以提高我们的词汇量。
The doctor suggested eating more fruits and vegetables for better health.
医生建议多吃水果和蔬菜以获得更好的健康。
The coach suggested practicing every day to become better at basketball.
教练建议每天练习以在篮球上变得更好。

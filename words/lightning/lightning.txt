lightning ['laɪtnɪŋ]
n. 闪电：The tree was struck by lightning. 这棵树被闪电击中。 <br> adj. 闪电的，迅速的：at lightning speed. 以闪电般的速度。
The lightning flashed across the sky, illuminating the dark clouds.
闪电划过天空，照亮了乌云。
The lightning struck the tree, causing it to catch fire.
闪电击中了树，导致它着火了。
The lightning-fast runner won the race easily.
闪电般的跑者轻松赢得了比赛。

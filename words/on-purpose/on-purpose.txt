on purpose ['pɜːpəs]
故意：Someone set thee fire on purpose. 有人故意纵火。
The stubborn child refused to apologize on purpose, despite knowing he was wrong.
固执的孩子故意不肯道歉，即使他知道自己错了。
The careless driver ran a red light on purpose, putting himself and others in danger.
粗心的司机故意闯红灯，使自己和他人都处于危险之中。
The cunning thief hid the stolen jewelry on purpose, hoping to avoid detection.
狡猾的小偷故意藏起了偷来的珠宝，希望逃避追捕。

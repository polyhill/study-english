power ['paʊə(r)]
n. 力，力量：Knowledge is power. 知识就是力量。<br>n. 权力；政权：The party has been in power for twenty years. 这个政党执政有20年了。
The solar panels harnessed the power of the sun to generate electricity.
太阳能电池利用太阳能发电。
Knowledge is power; the more you learn, the stronger you become.
知识就是力量；你学得越多，你就变得越强大。
Good leaders use their power to help others and make positive changes.
好的领导者利用他们的权力来帮助他人并做出积极的改变。

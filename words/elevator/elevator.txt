elevator ['elɪveɪtə(r)]
n. 电梯：I took the elevator to the eighth floor. 我乘坐电梯到八楼。
When the elevator doors open, we step inside and press the button for our floor.
当电梯门打开时，我们走进去并按下我们要去的楼层按钮。
If the elevator stops suddenly, it can be a little scary.
如果电梯突然停下来，会有点吓人。
Elevators are very helpful for people who have difficulty walking.
电梯对那些行动不便的人非常有帮助。

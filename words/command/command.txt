command [kə'mɑːnd] 
n. 命令；Run when I give the command. 我下令时就跑。<br>v. 命令：The officer commanded his men to shoot. 军官命令他手下射击。
The captain's command was to sail north.
船长的命令是向北航行。
The software can execute commands very quickly.
这款软件可以非常快地执行命令。
The coach commanded the team to practice harder.
教练命令球队更加努力地训练。

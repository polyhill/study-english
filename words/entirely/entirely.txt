entirely [ɪn'taɪəli]
adv. 完全地，全部地：That's an entirely different question. 那完全是另外一个问题。
The book is entirely based on historical facts.
这本书完全基于历史事实。
The teacher's explanation was entirely clear and easy to understand.
老师的解释完全清晰易懂。
The company's success is entirely due to the hard work of its employees.
公司的成功完全归功于员工的努力工作。

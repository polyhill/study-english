tip [tɪp]
n. 尖端；顶端：the tip of your nose. 鼻尖。<br>n. 提示；指点：He gave me some useful tips on how to choose a shirt. 他给了我一些选衬衫的提示。<br>n. 小费：The star gave the waiter a tip. 这个明星给了侍者消费。<br>v. 给...小费：She tipped the waitress 5 dollars. 她给了女侍者5美元小费。
A helpful tip for learning new words is to use flashcards.
学习新单词的一个有用建议是使用闪卡。
Remember this tip: stay organized to keep track of your tasks.
记住这个建议：保持有条理，以便跟踪你的任务。
Tip the waiter to show appreciation for good service.
给服务员小费以表达对良好服务的感谢。

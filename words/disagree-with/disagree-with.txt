disagree with
与…意见不一：He disagree with my statement. 他不同意我的说法。
The scientists disagree with the government's assessment of the environmental risks.
科学家们不同意政府对环境风险的评估。
I disagree with the notion that all reality is subjective.
我不同意所有现实都是主观的观点。
The evidence suggests that the witness's testimony is unreliable, and I disagree with the jury's verdict.
证据表明目击者的证词不可靠，我不同意陪审团的裁决。

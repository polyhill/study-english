make use of [meɪk juːz əv]
利用：We will make good use of our time. 我们将好好利用我们的时间。
He decided to make use of his artistic talent to create a beautiful painting.
他决定利用他的艺术天赋创作一幅美丽的画。
Make use of every opportunity to practice speaking English.
利用每一个机会练习说英语。
They made use of recycled materials to build the project.
他们利用回收材料建造这个项目。

block [blɒk]
n. 街区；木块；石块；大楼；阻塞(物)；障碍(物)<br>v. 阻塞；阻挡
Our apartment is in the next block, just a short walk away.
我们的公寓在下一个街区，走几步路就到了。
She felt a creative block and couldn't think of any new ideas for her project.
她感到创作遇到瓶颈，无法想到新的项目点子。
The road was blocked by a fallen tree after the storm.
暴风雨后，倒下的树挡住了道路。

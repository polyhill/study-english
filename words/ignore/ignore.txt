ignore [ɪɡ'nɔː(r)]
vt. 无视，对……不理会；不理睬：He ignored my advice. 他不理会我的建议。
The wise student ignored the taunts of his classmates and focused on his studies.
明智的学生无视了同学的嘲笑，专心学习。
The loyal friend ignored the gossip and remained supportive of his friend through thick and thin.
忠诚的朋友无视了流言蜚语，始终支持他的朋友。
The scientist ignored the skepticism of his colleagues and continued his groundbreaking research.
科学家无视了同事的怀疑，继续进行他的开创性研究。

expression [ɪk'spreʃn]
n. 表达；词语；表示；表达：a polite expression. 礼貌用语
He had a confused expression on his face when he saw the math problem.
当他看到那道数学题时，他脸上露出了困惑的表情。
It's important to use the right expression when writing a formal letter.
在写正式信件时使用正确的表达很重要。
His expression of gratitude was heartfelt and sincere.
他表达的感激之情真诚而发自内心。

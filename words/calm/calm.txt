calm [kɑːm] 
adj. 镇静的；沉着的：a calm sea 风平浪静的大海<br>v. (使)平静；(使)镇静：He tried to calm himself. 他试图让自己平静下来。
After the storm, the calm returned, bringing peace to the troubled town.
风暴过后，平静恢复，为这个陷入困境的小镇带来了和平。
The children's calm and focused faces showed that they were ready to learn.
孩子们平静而专注的脸庞表明他们准备好了学习。
The lake's calm surface reflected the towering mountains in the distance.
湖面平静地映照着远处的雄伟山脉。

suffering ['sʌfərɪŋ]
n. 痛苦；苦难：Death may put an end to his suffering. 死或许可以结束他的痛苦。
The war-torn country endured immense suffering, its people struggling to survive.
饱经战火的国家经历了巨大的苦难，人民为生存而挣扎。
The child's illness brought great suffering to the family, their hearts filled with worry.
孩子的疾病给家庭带来了巨大的痛苦，他们的心充满了担忧。
The world's suffering is a constant reminder of the need for compassion and understanding.
世界的苦难是不断提醒人们需要同情和理解的。
